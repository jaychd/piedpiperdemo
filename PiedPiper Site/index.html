About Pied Piper

Pied Piper was founded by Richard Hendricks in 2014 with a revolutionary compression algorithm. His original intention was to build an application that allowed musicians and songwriters to scan all music and see if they were infringing on copyrighted material.

Once he realized that his lossless compression algorithm scored a 2.89 Weissman score and later a 5.2 in a demonstration at TechCrunch Disrupt, he pursued his dream to start Pied Piper, a company that offered a once in a lifetime idea that could be worth billions.

His algorithm would also be used in a new video chat software called Piper Chat. Piper Chat offered amazing quality video conferencing over speeds as low as 3G without pixelation or quality degredation.

Pied Piper is now the bleeding edge of compression software in the market. If you want a platform that can compress all your data without any quality degredation, then Pied Piper is for you!
